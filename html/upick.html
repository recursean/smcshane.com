uPick

uPick is an iOS app written in Swift designed to help users find local bars and restaurants around them.

Stress less, eat more!

Simple

Just select your dining criteria and allow uPick to pick the place for you!

User Control

Don't like what the app picks for you? uPick gives you the power to choose from a list of places that meet your criteria.

Favorites

Create an account to save your favorite restaurants for later!
